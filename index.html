Why Cyber Security Awareness Matters

In today's interconnected world, cyber security is no longer a luxury but a necessity. Cyber criminals are becoming increasingly sophisticated, targeting businesses and individuals with phishing, ransomware, and data breaches.

Being cyber security conscious means adopting a proactive approach to digital safety, reducing the risk of human error, and ensuring compliance with regulations such as GDPR and CCPA.
